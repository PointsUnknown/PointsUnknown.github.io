About

Spatial training, paired with journalism, can serve as the missing “integrator” of data and the real world—providing lessons that travel beyond the boroughs of New York. The commoditization of spatial visualization tools can extend journalistic practice, helping reporters better understand and tell stories. These same tools expose the invisible spaces, forces and environments that architecture, urban design and planning students must engage, navigate and learn to represent as part of their spatial toolkit.
